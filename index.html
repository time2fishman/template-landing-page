Get to the NVZ line

Most points are won at the Non-Volley Zone line
Playing at the Kitchen line:
1. takes time away from your opponents
2. cuts down on the angles that your opponents have
3. allows you to hit down on the pickleball

Bend those knees

Getting low & staying low results in:
1. Improved Balance and Stability
2. Increased Power
3. Better Reach
4. Improved Reaction Time

Ball up, paddle down

1. The overhead is likely to be aimed at your feet, beat the ball to the spot by having your paddle low
2. Mimic a volleyball player digging a spike. Bend your knees and put that paddle down, readying to absorb contact
3. Any ball sent at chest or head level is likely to go out. There's rarely a need to defend a shot coming chest high
